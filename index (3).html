🛠️ Urge ko control karne ke 5 tareeke

🫁
1. 4-7-8 Breathing
4 sec saans andar lo, 7 sec roko, 8 sec dheere chhodo. 4 baar repeat. Urge ki energy turant girti hai.
🏃
2. 5-Minute Rule (Move!)
Khade ho jao — 20 pushups, ya thande paani se muh dho, ya bahar walk pe niklo. Body busy = mann shaant.
🧊
3. Urge Surfing
Urge ek lehar (wave) hai. Use rok-tok mat karo, bas dekho. 10-15 min mein apne aap chali jaati hai.
📵
4. Trigger Hatao
Phone door rakho, room se bahar jao, ya kisi dost ko call karo. Jis cheez ne trigger kiya use turant badlo.
🎯
5. Apna "Why" yaad karo
Streak, goal aur jo insaan banna chahte ho usse yaad karo. 10 din ki mehnat 1 minute mein barbaad mat karo.
